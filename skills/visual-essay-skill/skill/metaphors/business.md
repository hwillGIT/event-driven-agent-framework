# Metaphor Engine: BUSINESS / STRATEGY

**Concept**: Mapping corporate growth, time, execution, and complexity to tangible visual archetypes.
**Use When**: Explaining roadmaps, quarterly goals, market fit, team alignment, or abstract strategy.

## Visual Archetypes (Select One)

### 1. The Roadmap (Time & Sequence)
*   **Concept**: Linear progress, horizons, milestones.
*   **Visuals**: `Winding highway`, `Railroad tracks`, `Suspension bridge`, `Stepping stones across river`, `Mountain trail`.
*   **Use for**: Product launches, Timelines, History.

### 2. The Flywheel (Momentum & Growth)
*   **Concept**: Cycles that build energy, self-reinforcing loops.
*   **Visuals**: `Heavy industrial turbine`, `Gyroscope`, `Perpetual motion machine`, `Orbiting planets`, `Centrifuge`.
*   **Use for**: Viral growth, Retention loops, Compound interest.

### 3. The Iceberg (Hidden Complexity)
*   **Concept**: Visible results vs. invisible effort.
*   **Visuals**: `Iceberg (90% underwater)`, `Tree roots (deep underground)`, `Skyscraper foundation`, `Theater backstage`.
*   **Use for**: Risk management, Backend engineering, Culture.

### 4. The Ecosystem (Network & Market)
*   **Concept**: Interconnected entities, symbiosis.
*   **Visuals**: `Coral reef`, `Mycelium network`, `Constellation of stars`, `Busy city intersection`, `Circuit board`.
*   **Use for**: Platforms, Market dynamics, Community.

### 5. The Funnel (Filtering & Selection)
*   **Concept**: Narrowing down options, conversion.
*   **Visuals**: `Hourglass`, `Industrial hopper`, `Gold panning sieve`, `Distillery equipment`, `Tornado vortex`.
*   **Use for**: Sales pipelines, Hiring, Data processing.

### 6. The Balance (Trade-offs)
*   **Concept**: Weighing options, leverage.
*   **Visuals**: `Antique scales`, `Seesaw`, `Lever and fulcrum`, `Tightrope walker`.
*   **Use for**: Risk vs Reward, Cost vs Speed, Decision making.

### 7. The Pyramid (Hierarchy & Foundation)
*   **Concept**: Building blocks, dependencies.
*   **Visuals**: `Stone pyramid`, `Jenga tower`, `Layered cake`, `Mountain peak`.
*   **Use for**: Maslow's hierarchy, Tech stacks, Brand values.

### 8. The Matrix (Classification)
*   **Concept**: 2x2 Quadrants, Intersection.
*   **Visuals**: `Window pane`, `Crosshairs`, `Compass rose`, `Four seasons garden`.
*   **Use for**: SWOT analysis, Eisenhower matrix, Competitive landscape.

## Visual Vocabulary (General)
`shipping containers`, `cranes`, `lighthouses`, `relay batons`, `compasses`, `blueprints`, `puzzle pieces`, `keys`, `doors`.

## Special Logic: TEXT & LABELS
1.  **Do NOT** try to render text lists (e.g., "Q1, Q2, Q3") inside the image pixels.
2.  **DO** use visual metaphors for quantity:
    *   Instead of "30% growth", show a sapling becoming a tree.
    *   Instead of "3 Steps", show a staircase with 3 steps.
3.  **Overlay**: Place specific labels in the **LOCKED Text** layer.
